Frequently Asked Questions

Why is MacVit asking for system permissions?

MacVit requires certain system permissions to monitor your Mac's hardware and system information. These permissions are necessary to display accurate CPU usage, memory consumption, disk activity, and temperature data. All monitoring is done locally on your device - no data is sent to external servers.

How do I grant necessary permissions to MacVit?

Go to System Preferences > Security & Privacy > Privacy tab. Look for "Developer Tools" or "Full Disk Access" and ensure MacVit is checked. You may also need to grant permissions under "Accessibility" for complete system monitoring.

Is MacVit consuming too much battery or CPU?

MacVit is designed to be lightweight and efficient. If you notice high CPU usage, try reducing the monitoring frequency in settings. The app typically uses less than 1% CPU and minimal battery when running in the background.

Why are some monitoring features not working?

Some features may require additional permissions or may not be available on older Mac models. Temperature monitoring, for example, requires specific hardware sensors. Check that you've granted all necessary permissions and that your Mac model supports the feature.

Can I customize the dashboard layout?

Yes! You can customize which monitoring cards appear on your dashboard through the app preferences. You can also adjust update intervals and notification thresholds for different system metrics.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Use the contact form below to report bugs or request new features. Please include your macOS version, Mac model, and a detailed description of the issue or feature request.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions:

MacVit won't launch:

  • Ensure your macOS version meets the minimum requirements
  • Try restarting your Mac and launching the app again
  • Check if Gatekeeper is blocking the app in Security & Privacy settings

Inaccurate system information:

  • Grant full system access permissions
  • Restart the app to refresh system connections
  • Some information may take a few seconds to update initially

App crashes or freezes:

  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • Reset app preferences in Settings
  • Contact support with crash logs if the issue persists
